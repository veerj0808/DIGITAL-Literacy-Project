# Social Media Do's and Don'ts 

## Do's
- Do think before posting anything online.
- Do keep your personal information safe.
- Do use respectful and polite language.
- Do share useful and positive content.
- Do check facts before sharing news.

## Don'ts
- Don't share fake news or rumors.
- Don't post anything offensive or harmful.
- Don't overshare personal details like address or phone number.
- Don't engage in online fights or arguments.
- Don't waste too much time on social media.
